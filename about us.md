# About ACE Electrical Limited

## Our Story

ACE Electrical Limited was established in 2008 and has been committed to becoming a global leader in smart care and pet needs solutions. We strive to weave a safe, convenient, and heartwarming protective network for families and their loved ones worldwide.

With stringent quality control standards and user-centric innovative designs, our products have been exported to numerous countries and regions, serving families across diverse locations.
